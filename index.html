About the Challenge

I'm Maria Hendrikx, a passionate developer embarking on an ambitious challenge: building 100 different applications in 100 days. This challenge is about pushing my boundaries, learning new technologies, and documenting the entire journey.

Each app will be unique, covering different domains, technologies, and complexity levels. From simple utilities to complex web applications, I'll share the entire process - from idea to deployment.

Challenge Rules

  • Build 100 unique applications
  • Document the process for each app
  • Learn at least one new technology per app
  • Share code and insights openly
  • Complete within 100 days
