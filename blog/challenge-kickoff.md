# Starting the 100 App Challenge

**Date:** Day 1  
**Category:** Kickoff

The journey begins! Read about why I decided to take on this challenge and what I hope to achieve.

---

## Why This Challenge?

I've been a developer for several years now, and like many of us, I often find myself stuck in a comfort zone. I work with the same technologies, build similar types of applications, and while I learn continuously, it's often incremental rather than transformative.

The 100 App Challenge is my way of breaking out of that comfort zone. By committing to building 100 different apps, I force myself to:

- **Start without overthinking** - No more spending weeks planning the perfect architecture
- **Learn technologies I've been avoiding** - Rust, Go, Flutter, you name it
- **Ship code regularly** - Done is better than perfect
- **Embrace imperfection** - Not every app needs to be production-ready
- **Build a diverse portfolio** - From CLI tools to full-stack web apps

## The Rules

To keep myself accountable and ensure this challenge is meaningful, I've set some ground rules:

1. Build 100 unique applications (no clones or minor variations)
2. Document the process for each app through blog posts
3. Learn at least one new technology per app
4. Share all code openly on GitHub
5. Take my time - quality over speed (unlimited timeline)

## What to Expect

Throughout this challenge, I'll be sharing:

- Detailed blog posts about each app I build
- Code snippets and technical challenges I encounter
- Lessons learned and mistakes made
- Resources and tutorials that helped me along the way
- Progress updates and reflections on the journey

## The Apps

I plan to build apps across various categories:

- **Web Applications** (React, Vue, Next.js, vanilla JS)
- **Mobile Apps** (React Native, Flutter)
- **CLI Tools and Utilities**
- **Browser Extensions**
- **Simple Games**
- **Data Visualization Apps**
- **APIs and Microservices**
- **Automation Scripts**

## Join Me on This Journey

This challenge isn't just about me - it's about community and shared learning. I encourage you to:

- Follow along with my progress
- Build your own apps alongside me
- Share your feedback and suggestions
- Contribute ideas for apps I should build
- Learn from my mistakes and successes

## Let's Get Started!

The timer starts now. 100 projects, unlimited time. Let's see what we can build together!

Stay tuned for my next post where I'll share my development setup and the tools I'll be using throughout this challenge.
